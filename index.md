## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Dengar+. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application for meeting transcription and summarization.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Audio Recordings
- **What:** Audio recordings of your meetings and conversations
- **Storage:** Stored **locally on your device only** in the app's Documents directory
- **Transmission:** Temporarily uploaded to our backend server, which forwards to OpenAI for transcription processing
- **Retention:** Audio files are **immediately deleted** from our servers after processing completes
- **Your Control:** You can delete recordings anytime from within the app

### 2.2 Device Identifier
- **What:** Anonymous device UUID (generated by iOS)
- **Purpose:** To manage free trial quotas (5 transcriptions per device) and Premium subscription validation
- **Storage:** Stored on our secure backend server (AWS Lightsail)
- **No Personal Data:** Device ID cannot be linked to your identity

### 2.3 Usage Tracking
- **What:** Number of transcriptions used (quota management)
- **Purpose:** To enforce free tier limits and track Premium subscription monthly usage
- **Data Collected:** Device ID, transcription count, quota remaining, monthly reset date
- **Storage:** Persistent backend database (SQLite)

### 2.4 Purchase Information
- **What:** In-App Purchase transaction IDs and subscription status
- **Purpose:** To verify Premium subscription ($4.99/month) and provide 30 monthly transcriptions
- **Processing:** Handled by Apple App Store; we only receive validation confirmation
- **Privacy:** Apple processes all payment information (we never see credit card details)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Core Functionality
- **Transcription:** Audio temporarily uploaded to our server → forwarded to OpenAI Whisper API → transcribed text returned to app → audio deleted from server
- **Summarization:** Transcripts sent to OpenAI GPT-4o-mini API for intelligent meeting summaries
- **Quota Management:**
  - **Free Tier:** 5 transcriptions total per device (lifetime)
  - **Premium Tier:** 30 transcriptions per month with $4.99/month subscription (resets monthly)

### 3.2 No Permanent Storage
- **Audio files:** Deleted immediately after transcription
- **Transcripts:** Stored **only on your device**, never on our servers
- **Summaries:** Stored **only on your device**, never on our servers

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

### 4.1 OpenAI (Transcription & Summarization)
- **Data Shared:** Audio recordings (temporary), transcripts (for summarization)
- **Purpose:** AI-powered transcription (Whisper API) and summarization (GPT-4o-mini API)
- **Privacy Policy:** [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy)
- **Data Retention:** OpenAI may temporarily store data for processing; see their policy for details
- **Security:** All communication encrypted via HTTPS

### 4.2 AWS Lightsail (Backend Hosting)
- **Data Shared:** Device ID, transcription count, subscription status
- **Purpose:** Proxy OpenAI API calls, manage quotas, validate subscriptions
- **Server Location:** AWS Singapore (ap-southeast-1)
- **Security:** HTTPS encryption, secure environment variables
- **No Audio Storage:** Audio files immediately deleted after processing

### 4.3 Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchases)
- **Data Shared:** Purchase transaction IDs
- **Purpose:** Subscription management and validation
- **Privacy Policy:** [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)
- **Payment Processing:** Handled entirely by Apple (we never access payment details)

---

## 5. Data Storage & Retention

### 5.1 On Your Device (Local Storage)
- **Audio recordings:** Stored in app Documents directory until you delete them
- **Transcripts:** Stored in CoreData database
- **Summaries:** Stored in CoreData database
- **User settings:** Stored in UserDefaults and Keychain

### 5.2 On Our Backend Servers (AWS Lightsail)
- **Device ID:** Stored to track quota usage
- **Transcription count:** Current usage for both free and premium users
- **Subscription data:** Expiry date, product ID, transaction ID
- **Monthly reset tracking:** Premium quota reset dates
- **Retention:** 90 days after last use (automatically purged)

### 5.3 What We DO NOT Store
- ❌ Your audio files (deleted immediately after processing)
- ❌ Your transcripts (local only)
- ❌ Your summaries (local only)
- ❌ Personal information (name, email, phone, etc.)
- ❌ Payment information (handled by Apple)

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
- **Encryption at Rest:** iOS security protects on-device data
- **Backend Security:** Secure AWS Lightsail infrastructure with environment variable protection
- **API Keys:** OpenAI API key stored securely in server environment variables
- **No Public Access:** Backend API requires device ID header (prevents unauthorized access)
- **Automatic Cleanup:** Temporary files deleted immediately after processing

## 12. Data Deletion Instructions

### To Delete Local Data:
1. **Delete Individual Recordings:**
   App → Recordings → Swipe left on recording → Delete

2. **Delete All Local Data:**
   Delete the app from your device
   iOS Settings → General → iPhone Storage → Dengar+ → Delete App

### To Delete Backend Data:
Email ashrafnaim81@gmail.com with:
- Subject: "Backend Data Deletion Request"
- Device ID (Settings → About in app)
- We'll confirm deletion within 7 business days

### To Cancel Subscription:
1. iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
2. Select "Dengar+"
3. Tap "Cancel Subscription"
4. **Note:** Remaining quota for current billing cycle stays active until expiration
