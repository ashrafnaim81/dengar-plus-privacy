## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Dengar+. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Audio Recordings
- **What:** Audio recordings of your meetings
- **Storage:** Stored **locally on your device only**
- **Transmission:** Sent to OpenAI for transcription processing (see Section 4)
- **Deletion:** You can delete recordings anytime from within the app

### 2.2 Usage Data
- **What:** Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., number of transcriptions, app crashes)
- **Purpose:** To improve app performance and user experience
- **Data:** Device ID (anonymized), app version, transcription count
- **Storage:** Stored on our secure backend server

### 2.3 Purchase Information
- **What:** In-App Purchase receipts for Premium subscription
- **Purpose:** To verify subscription status and provide Premium features
- **Processing:** Handled by Apple App Store (we only receive validation data)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Core Functionality
- **Transcription:** Audio sent to OpenAI Whisper API for speech-to-text conversion
- **Summarization:** Transcripts sent to OpenAI GPT-4o-mini for meeting summaries
- **Quota Tracking:** Device ID used to track free trial usage (3 transcriptions)

### 3.2 Service Improvement
- Anonymous analytics to understand feature usage
- Crash reports to fix bugs and improve stability

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

### 4.1 OpenAI (Transcription & Summarization)
- **Data Shared:** Audio recordings, transcripts
- **Purpose:** AI transcription and summarization
- **Privacy:** See [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy)

### 4.2 Railway (Backend Hosting)

- **Data Shared:** Purchase receipts
- **Purpose:** Subscription management
- **Privacy:** See [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

## 5. Data Storage

### 5.1 On Your Device
- All audio recordings stored in app's Documents directory
- User preferences and settings stored in UserDefaults
- CoreData database for recording metadata

### 5.2 On Our Servers
- Anonymous device ID
- Transcription usage count
- Subscription validation data
- **We DO NOT store:** Your audio files, transcripts, or personal information

## 6. Data Security

- **Backend:** Secure Railway cloud infrastructure
- **API Keys:** User-provided API keys encrypted in iOS Keychain

## 11. Data Deletion Instructions

To delete all your data:

1. **Delete Recordings:** Open app → Recordings → Swipe left on each recording → Delete
2. **Reset Usage:** Settings → Select "BYOK" plan (clears quota tracking)
3. **Cancel Subscription:** iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Cancel Dengar+
4. **Delete App:** Long-press app icon → Remove App → Delete App

This removes all local data. For backend data deletion, email us at ashrafnaim81@gmail.com.
