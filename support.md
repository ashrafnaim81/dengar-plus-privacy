## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

#### How do I start recording a meeting?
1. Open Dengar+ app
2. Tap "Start Recording" on the home screen
3. Give your recording a name (optional)
4. Tap the record button to begin
5. Make sure to grant microphone permissions when prompted

#### What devices are supported?
Dengar+ supports iPhone and iPad running iOS 16.0 or later.

---

### Free Plan & Quotas

#### How many free transcriptions do I get?
The Free Plan includes **5 lifetime transcriptions**. This quota never resets and is tracked per device.

#### What happens when I use all my free transcriptions?
You'll need to subscribe to Premium ($4.99/month) to continue transcribing. You can still:
- Play back existing recordings
- View existing transcripts and summaries
- Export existing content

---

### Premium Subscription

#### How much does Premium cost?
Premium costs **$4.99 USD per month** (prices may vary by region). You get 30 transcriptions per month that reset on your billing date.

#### How do I subscribe to Premium?
1. Go to **Settings** in the app
2. Under "Usage Plan", select **Premium**
3. Tap **"Subscribe Premium $4.99/month"**
4. Confirm with Face ID, Touch ID, or Apple ID password

#### How do I restore my subscription on a new device?
1. Go to **Settings** in the app
2. Tap **"Restore Purchases"** under Subscription section
3. Sign in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase
4. Your subscription will be restored automatically

#### How do I cancel my subscription?
1. Open **Settings** app on your iPhone
2. Tap your **name** at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Select **Dengar+**
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription**

Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

#### When does my monthly quota reset?
Your 30 transcriptions reset on your subscription renewal date each month. Check Settings in the app to see your reset date.

### Transcription & Recording

#### Why is my transcription not accurate?
Transcription accuracy depends on audio quality. For best results:
- Record in a **quiet environment**
- Speak **clearly and at moderate pace**
- Place your device **close to speakers**
- Avoid **background noise** and music
- Use an external microphone for large meetings

#### How long can I record?
There's no time limit on recordings. However, very long recordings (over 2 hours) may take longer to process.

#### What audio formats are supported?
Dengar+ records in M4A format. You can also import audio files in MP3, M4A, WAV, and other common formats.

#### Can I transcribe audio in languages other than English?
Yes! Dengar+ supports multiple languages including:
- English
- Bahasa Melayu
- And many others supported by OpenAI Whisper

### Export & Sharing

#### How do I export my transcript?
1. Open the recording
2. View the transcript
3. Tap the **Share** button
4. Choose **PDF** or **DOCX** format
5. Share via AirDrop, email, or save to Files

#### Can I add meetings to my calendar?
Yes! After generating a summary, tap "Add to Calendar" to create a calendar event with the meeting summary and action items.

### Privacy & Data

#### Is my data secure?
Yes. Your recordings are stored **locally on your device**. Audio is only sent for processing when you request transcription, and we **don't store your audio files** on our servers after processing.

#### Where is my data stored?
- **Audio recordings:** Locally on your device
- **Transcripts:** Locally on your device
- **Summaries:** Locally on your device
- **Usage tracking:** Anonymous device ID on our secure servers

#### How do I delete my data?
**Local data:**
- Swipe left on any recording to delete it
- Or delete the app to remove all local data

**Backend data:**
- Email us at ashrafnaim81@gmail.com with subject "Data Deletion Request"
- Include your device ID (found in Settings → About)

### Troubleshooting

#### App is not working / crashes
1. Force close the app and reopen
2. Restart your device
3. Check for app updates in the App Store
4. If problem persists, email us with details

#### Transcription failed
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you have remaining quota
- Try again with a shorter recording
- Make sure audio is clear and audible

#### Purchase not recognized
1. Go to Settings → "Restore Purchases"
2. Make sure you're signed in with the correct Apple ID
3. Wait a few minutes and try again
4. Contact us if the issue persists

#### Microphone not working
1. Go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Microphone
2. Ensure Dengar+ is enabled
3. Restart the app
